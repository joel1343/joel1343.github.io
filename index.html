Basketball For Absolute Dummies:

Basketball is a worldwide sport played by many people. This section of the website will be for those who are brand knew to the sport and want to know more about it.

Basketball is a sport played by more that 250 million people around the world. According to the Worldatlas.com it is the 7th most popular sport in the world with more than 800 million followers of the sport. The game is made up of two teams going against each other with the objective to successfully shoot a basketball into a hoop located 10 feet off the ground. The teams consist of 5 players on each team on the court, each player having their own positions.
